Limbo

Role: User Interface Designer, Developer

Context: Professional Portfolio Commission

Timeline: Jan 2025

Tools: Figma, Miro

A digital showcase capturing an artist’s vision and portfolio.

01 Project Background

This project involved designing and developing an online portfolio for a contemporary artist. The goal was to create a clean and minimalist website that highlights their artwork, tells their story, and facilitates engagement with clients. By emphasizing simplicity and elegance, the portfolio serves as a canvas that enhances the impact of the artist’s creative expressions.

02 Key Features

  • Gallery of Artwork: A responsive and visually engaging display of the artist's work.
  • About the Artist: A section that narrates the artist's story and creative journey.
  • Contact and Inquiry Form: An easy-to-use form for inquiries and collaborations.
  • Mobile-Friendly Design: Optimised for viewing on any device, ensuring accessibility.
  • Minimalist Aesthetic: A clean and elegant design that lets the artwork shine.

03 Process

Technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript

  • Understanding the Vision: Collaborated with LIMBO organisers to align the design with their vision of life by the sea and its cultural significance.
  • Wireframing and Designing: Designed Figma wireframes with an ocean-inspired theme reflecting LIMBO's connection to fishing communities and surfers.
  • Development: Built a responsive portfolio using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, focusing on usability and visual appeal.
  • Interactive Elements: Added animations and transitions to create an engaging and immersive experience.
  • Testing and Launch: Tested on multiple devices and browsers to ensure a polished and consistent final product.

04 Key Features

  • Location-based Forecast: Shows weather for your current location.
  • Unit Conversion: Toggle between Celsius and Fahrenheit.
  • Hourly and Weekly Forecast: Provides detailed temperature trends.
  • Clean UI: Minimalistic design focused on usability.
