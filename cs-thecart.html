The Cart

Role: iOS Developer

Context: Commisioned Collaboration

Timeline: Oct 2024 - Ongoing

Tools: Swift, API

01 Project Background

The Cart is a universal shopping cart creating a central space for you to save items as you browse, allowing you to make a considered purchase decision.

02 Key Features

  • Save with a Tap: Quickly save items from social media or any website using seamless app integration.
  • Organised Carts: Store and organise saved items in dedicated carts for easy comparison.
  • Personalised Shopping Experience: Leverage AI to curate a unique ‘Explore’ page tailored to user preferences and styles.
  • Considered Purchase Decisions: Empower users to make thoughtful buying decisions with individualised product-focused carts.
  • Streamlined Checkout: Enable quick navigation to purchase items directly from saved carts.

03 Process

Technologies: Swift
